Thieboudienne is considered to be the national dish of Senegal. Thieboudienne can be made with an assortment of different vegetables. Smoked fish is one the ingredients which adds great flavor.

Ingredients

  • Marinated fish
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 red onion , finely diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • ½ Maggi cube (optional)
  • Vegetable oil
  • smoked fish fish

Steps

  • Grind the parsley, garlic, scotch bonnet, bullion black pepper.
  • Make the tomato sauce.
  • Add the Maggi cube, bay leaves, nététou, smoked fish.
  • Remove the fish and add vegetables
  • Cook the rice
  • Season with vegetable oil and the crumbled Maggi cube.
  • place the rice on a nice dish and arrange the vegetables and fish on top.
