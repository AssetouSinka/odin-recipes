Burkina is a beverage made from ground millet and pasteurized milk It original name is “Degue” it is very nutritious and has lots of health benefits It is usually served chilled in a cup or sold bottled.>

Ingredients

  • Millet
  • fresh cow milk
  • sugar
  • salt (optional)

Steps

  1. wash your millet and later soak it over night.
  2. drain the water out of it and grind it into a rough or course texture.
  3. Heat up water and boil the millet
  4. Fetch the millet into a sieve and use your hands to roll it in tiny balls.
  5. Pour the tiny balls into a colander.
  6. When the water is hot, lower the colander onto the steamy pot.
  7. Cover the colander tightly and allow steam from the hot water.
  8. When the tiny balls become soft and cooked, pour into bowl.
  9. You can add the cow milk or prepare a milk drink.
  10. Add sugar to taste then refrigerate.
